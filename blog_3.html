Essential JavaScript concepts

As a reward for completing the first course, I got an offer on signing up as a trial member, and go take one more course for free. A great deal! I guess this is how you get students to enroll, right Per (CEO at Scrimba)?

What did I learn?.

This is what this course teach you:

  • Position fixed (with setTimeout)
  • new ways of useing document.getElement....
  • buttons, forms and storing form data
  • Toggle
  • for of loops
  • rendering out data
  • import and export
  • includes and duplicates
  • Queryselector
  • ForEach
  • Booleans and flipping them
And of course, much much more.

I have now come to the conclution that what I thought I knew about programming with Javascript was so much less than it actually was. So much to learn!! But with Scrimba, it gets so clear and "easy" to figure it all out! What we make in this module is:

  • A cookie consent
  • A meme picker
  • Twitter clone
  • Solo project: An restaurant ordering App

The restaurant app was a challenging project, but quite fun and educating. You can take a look at my finished app if you want :)
